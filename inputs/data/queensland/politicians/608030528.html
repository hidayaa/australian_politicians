Fenlon, Gary Bernard

Personal


Birth Date: 30 October 1954 (Rockhampton, Queensland, Australia)

Parents: Bernard Conleth Fenlon and Doreen May (nee Scott)

Family: Dianne Small in December 1989, 1 daughter

Education: St Mary's Convent and Christian Brothers High School, Rockhampton; BBus (Essex University); MA(Adelaide University); DipEd

Career

Industrial advocate and teacher at high school and university; Served on Griffith University Council from 1990 to 1996

Party Positions:

  1. (ALP) Member, Ministerial Legislative Committees: Deputy Premier, Treasurer and Infrastructure; Child Safety; Local Government, Planning and Sport; and Transport and Main Roads, 28 Sep 2006 - 21 Mar 2009
  2. (ALP) Member, Ministerial Legislative Committees: State Development, Employment and Industrial Relations; and Primary Industries and Fisheries, 28 Sep 2006 - 21 Mar 2009
  3. (ALP) Member, Ministerial Legislative Committees: Natural Resources and Water and North Queensland; and Education and Training and the Arts, 28 Sep 2006 - 21 Mar 2009

Parliamentary Representation:
