Personal


Birth Date: 22 March 1973 (Brisbane, Queensland, Australia)

Career

Soldier in the Regular Army, 7th Signal Regiment (EW)


Parliamentary Representation:

House Party Electorate From To Elected/Departure Reason
Assembly PHON Tablelands 13 Jun 1998 6 Feb 1999
Assembly IND Tablelands 6 Feb 1999 17 Feb 2001

Speeches: All Speeches

Parliamentary Service

Description From To
Pauline Hanson's One Nation Shadow Spokesperson for Sport, Racing and Tourism; Mines, Energy and Regional Development 4 Nov 1998 6 Feb 1999
