Roberts, Hon Neil Stuart

Personal


Birth Date: 3 January 1955 (Charleville, Queensland, Australia)

Family: 2 daughters

Education: Electrical Fitter and Mechanic; Bachelor of Business; Master of Business Administration (QUT); GAICD

Career

Industrial Advocate for Electrical Trades Union

Party Positions:

  1. (ALP) Member of ALP since 1981
  2. (ALP) State Council Delegate Lilley (1982-1994)
  3. (ALP) State Conference Delegate (1988, 1991, 1994)
  4. (ALP) Campaign Director, Lilley (1993, 1996); Nudgee (1989, 1992); Northgate Ward (1994); Eagle Farm Ward (1991)
  5. (ALP) Member Rules Committee (1991-2002)
  6. (ALP) Member Economic Management Policy Committee (1991-1994)
  7. (ALP) President and Secretary of Various Party Units

Parliamentary Representation:

House Party Electorate From To Elected/Departure Reason
Assembly ALP Nudgee 15 Jul 1995 23 Mar 2012 Did not contest

Speeches: Inaugural | All Speeches
