Power, Laurel Jean

Personal


Birth Date: 7 December 1953 (Augathella, Queensland, Australia)

Education: Southport State School; Southport State High School; Kelvin Grove CAE; Diploma of Teaching

Career

School teacher

Party Positions:

  1. (ALP) Delegate, Mansfield Electorate Executive Committee
  2. (ALP) Federal Divisional Executive for Fadden
  3. (ALP) State Council Delegate, Fadden
  4. (ALP) Campaign Director, Moreton, 1986
  5. (ALP) Member, Ministerial Legislative Committees: Premier; Health; Education; Employment, Training and Industrial Relations; Business, Industry and Regional Development, 1989 - 1992
  6. (ALP) Member, Ministerial Legislative Committees: Premier; Employment; Training and Industrial Relations, 1992 - 1995
