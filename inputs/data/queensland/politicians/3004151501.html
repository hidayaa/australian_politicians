Dollin, Robert Henry (Bob)

Personal


Birth Date: 6 December 1928 (Kyogle, New South Wales, Australia)

Family: 1 son, 3 daughters

Career

Manager in the timber industry

Party Positions:

  1. (ALP) Federal Divisional delegate
  2. (ALP) State Council delegate
  3. (ALP) Member, Ministerial Legislative Committees: Lands; Primary Industries; Minerals and Energy; Justice and Consumer Affairs, 1990 - 1995
  4. (ALP) Member, Ministerial Legislative Committees: Transport; Employment, Training and Public Service; Emergency Services and Consumer Affairs; Primary Industries and Racing, Sep 1995 - Feb 1996

Parliamentary Representation:

House Party Electorate From To Elected/Departure Reason
Assembly ALP Maryborough 2 Dec 1989 13 Jun 1998

Parliamentary Service

Description From To
Member, Estimates Committee F 31 Mar 1995 13 Jun 1995
Member, Estimates Committee F 28 Apr 1994 23 Jun 1994
Member, Premier's Rural and Northern Task Force May 1992 Jul 1995
Member, Select Committee on Travelsafe 10 May 1990 19 May 1998
Member, Printing Committee Mar 1990 Sep 1992

Additional Information

Notes: Interests include fishing, gardening and horse riding
