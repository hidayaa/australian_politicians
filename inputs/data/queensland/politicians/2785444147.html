Hollis, Raymond Keith (Ray)

Personal


Birth Date: 30 January 1940 (London, England)

Family: 4 children

Career

Transport and distribution business; Insurance salesperson; Victorian Corrective Services; railway worker, cook and paper mill operator

Party Positions:

  1. (ALP) Member of the Australian Labor Party, since 1986
  2. (ALP) Member, Ministerial Legislative Committee: Administrative Services, 1991 - 1992
  3. (ALP) Member, Ministerial Legislative Committees: Justice and Corrective Services; Police and Emergency Services, Family Services and Aboriginal and Islander Affairs, Dec 1989 - Aug 1992
  4. (ALP) Member, Ministerial Legislative Committees: Business Industry and Regional Development, Sep 1992 - Feb 1996
  5. (ALP) Member, Ministerial Legislative Committees: Family and Community Services; Education, Sep 1995 - Feb 1996

Parliamentary Representation:

Assembly ALP Redcliffe 2 Dec 1989 21 Jul 2005
