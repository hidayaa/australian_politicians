Clark, Elizabeth Anne (Liddy)

Personal


Birth Date: 6 November 1953 (Adelaide, South Australia, Australia)

Career

Industrial Officer with the Media, Entertainment and Arts Alliance; Worked for over 20 years in the arts industry as an actor, director and producer

Party Positions:

  1. (ALP) MEC Delegate and EEC Delegate, 1996 - 1998
  2. (ALP) President, Queensland Labor Women's Organisation, 1999 - 2000
  3. (ALP) Member, Ministerial Legislative Committees: Premier; State Development; Transport and Main Roads; Tourism, Racing and Fair Trading, Mar 2001 - 2006

Parliamentary Representation:

Assembly ALP Clayfield 17 Feb 2001 9 Sep 2006
