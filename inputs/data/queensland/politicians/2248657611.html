Fraser, Andrew Peter

Personal


Birth Date: 15 September 1976 (Proserpine, Queensland, Australia)

Parents: Peter Fraser and Jeanne (nee Langfield)

Family: Therese O'Reilly on 21 September 2002, 1 son, 1 daughter

Education: Proserpine State High School Griffith University; Bachelor of Laws/Bachelor of Commerce

Career

Campaign Coordinator, Australian Labor Party, National Secretariat

Party Positions:

  1. (ALP) Former President, Secretary of Mount Coot-tha State Electorate Council
  2. (ALP) Former Vice-President Brisbane Federal Electoral Council
  3. (ALP) State Conference Delegate for Brisbane
  4. (ALP) Former Vice-President of Queensland Young Labor
  5. (ALP) Former Campaign Coordinator, ALP National Secretariat
  6. (ALP) National Organiser, ALP National Secretariat
  7. (ALP) State Organiser, ALP State Office

Parliamentary Representation:
