Hobbs, Howard William Thomas

Personal


Birth Date: 22 January 1950 (Brisbane)

Parents: William Howard Foley Hobbs and Dorothea Knowles (nee Davidson)

Family: Marilyn Ann Hooper on 9 January 1971, 1 son, 1 daughter

Education: Tambo Convent; Columboola State School; Downlands

Religion: Roman catholic

Career

Private Pilot; Grazier

Party Positions:

  1. (NP) Ministerial Legislative Committee, Premier, Treasurer and the Arts, Land Management, Finance, Environment, Conservation and Tourism, Local Government and Racing, Health , 1987 - 1989

Local Government Service: Chairman, Tambo Shire Council, 1980-1987. Councillor Tambo Shire Council 1975-1980. Executive Member of the Western Queensland and the South West Queensland Local Government Associations

Military Service: Military Service 49 RQR, Wacol


Parliamentary Representation:
