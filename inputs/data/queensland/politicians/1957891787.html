Gibbs, Hon Robert James (Bob)

Personal


Birth Date: 3 November 1946 (Brisbane, Queensland, Australia)

Parents: Cyril James Gibbs and Mavis Alexa (nee McCheane)

Family: Brenda Coombs on 10 April 1965, 1 son, 1 daughter

Religion: Church of England

Career

Boilermaker; Organiser, Miscellaneous Workers Union

Party Positions:

  1. (ALP) Elected full-time official of the Australian Labor Party, 1972
  2. (ALP) Assistant State Secretary, Australian Labor Party, 1974 - 1976
  3. (ALP) Queensland State President, Australian Labor Party, 1994 - 1996

Parliamentary Representation:

Assembly ALP Wolston 12 Nov 1977 19 Sep 1992
Assembly ALP Bundamba 19 Sep 1992 14 Dec 1999 Did not contest

Speeches: All Speeches
